It's Nothing to be Ashamed of...

Mental health, like physical health, is extremely important to our wellbeing & quality of life. Perhaps even more important, because when one suffers from mental illness, one tends to neglect their physical health. Something I, & millions of others can attest to. But all too often, because of the social stigmas surrounding mental illness, over 75% of people are compelled to neglect getting diagnosed or recieving the proper treatment, leading to obesity, poor hygiene, poverty, death, & more. But it doesn't have to be this way, & it shouldn't be this way. No one should be made to feel ashamed for something they have no control over. Most of the time, peoples mental problems stem from chemical imballances in the brain that are, at this point in time, treatable but not currable. Why should something nearly 1 billion people on earth live, & suffer with cause us to be ashamed? Two words, it shouldn't.
