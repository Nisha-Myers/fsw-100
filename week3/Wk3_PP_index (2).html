Why the Universal Health system?

Because Healthcare is a Human Right!

The U.S. ranks last on access to care, administrative efficiency, equity, and health care outcomes, but second on measures of care process. 1 in 10 people in the U.S. don't have access to healthcare. In fact, deaths from lack of health insurance now exceed those caused by many other common causes of death... such as kidney disease. In 2019 a Gallup poll found that 25% of Americans said they or a family member have delayed medical treatment for a serious illness due to the costs of medical care. An additional 8% reported delaying medical treatment for less serious illnesses.
