So, why have Conservatives given Liberals such a bad name?

Well, to answer this question, lets begin with the year 1968, when a debate was held between Liberal writer Gore Vidal & conservative thinker William F. Buckley. The hope was that these two members of opposing intellectual elites could prove to Americans living through tumultuous times, that political disagreements could in fact be civilized. Unfortunately, the hope for such an outcome didn't last very long. Rather than being capable of working through their differences, conservative Buckley & Liberal Vidal quickly descended into insults, & finally suing one another for defamation.
The story of the 1968 Vidal & Buckley debate opens a well regarded 2013 book titled "Predisposed" that introduced the general public to the field of political neuroscience. The authors of this book are a trio of political scientists at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln & Rice University. They argued that "if the differences between Liberals and Conservatives seem profound and even unbridgeable, it is because they are rooted in personality characteristics and biological predispositions." In other words, a persons political standing is rooted in one's personality & biology...

Overall, their research showed that while Conservatives desire security, predictability, & authority... Liberals are more comfortable with novelty, nuance, & complexity. Which in this authors opinion would explain why Liberals & Conservatives have a completely different requirements when it comes to forming an opinion on a particular situation. To put it simply: a Liberal is determined to know what beyond the surface of the water, while a Conservative is content with skipping rocks across it.
If you had hooked Vidal & Buckley up to a magnetic resonance imaging machine, & presented them with identical images, you would likely have seen differences in their brain, especially in the areas that process social and emotional information. The volume of Gray Matter, or Neural Cell Bodies, making up the Anterior Cingulate Cortex (an area that helps detect errors and resolve conflicts) tends to be larger in Liberals. And the amygdala (which is important for regulating emotions and evaluating threats) is larger in Conservatives. Proving why Liberals tend to be empathetic & considerate, & Conservatives are apathetic & self-serving.
